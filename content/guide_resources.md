# Resources page

1. Clinic Locators  
    1. [ineedana](https://www.ineedana.com/): A directory of vetted abortion clinics  
    2. [Abortion Finder](https://www.abortionfinder.org/): A directory of vetted abortion clinics  
2. Abortion funds and Practical Support Organizations  
    1. [National Network of Abortion Fund](https://abortionfunds.org/)s (NNAF): a network of nearly 100 abortion funds. You can search for abortion funds that cover your area or the area of your appointment.  
    2. [Chicago Abortion Fund:](https://www.chicagoabortionfund.org/) an abortion fund that provides financial and logistical support for people from the midwest or receiving their care in the midwest including residents of Illinois, Wisconsin, Nebraska, Arkansas and Iowa **or** anyone traveling to Illinois, Wisconsin, Iowa & Nebraska for care.  
        1. The Chicago Abortion Fund also has a program, [CARLA](https://www.carlaprogram.org/), specifically to support people seeking care in hospitals. CARLA is NOT restricted to the Midwest.  
    3. [National Abortion Federation:](https://prochoice.org/patients/naf-hotline/) The National Abortion Hotline is the largest national, toll-free, multi-lingual Hotline for abortion provider information and financial assistance in the U.S. and Canada. 

3. Practical Support Organizations  
    1. [BRIDGID Alliance](https://brigidalliance.org/): a practical support organization that provides support for later abortion care  
    2. [Apiary](https://apiaryps.org/pso-list): Apiary is a repository of practical support organizations. You can find one that covers the area of your appointment, or a national organization  
4. Abortion Story Telling  
    1. [Who Not When](https://whonotwhen.com/): an organization that provides information and advocacy for later abortion  
    2. [r/abortion](https://www.reddit.com/r/abortion/)  : a reddit page run by volunteers who provide compassionate responses to stories and questions about abortion care.  
    3. [Shout your abortion](https://shoutyourabortion.com/) : a repository of abortion stories  
    4. [We Testify](https://www.abortion.shop/mobile) : a repository of abortion stories 

5. Group Therapy    
    1. [Postpartum International After-Abortion support group](https://postpartum.net/group/post-abortion-support/): group therapy for people who have experienced abortion  
    2. [Post Partum International TMFR support group](https://postpartum.net/group/termination-for-medical-reasons-support-for-parents/): group therapy for people who have experienced termination for medical reasons.  
6. Mental Health Supports  
    1. [Exhale pro-voice](https://exhaleprovoice.org/): a non-judgemental after-abortion support text line    
    2. [Pro-Choice Therapists](https://www.prochoicetherapists.org/member-directory%20) : a directory of pro-choice therapists     
    3. [Termination For Medical Reasons Therapy](https://www.tfmrpsychologist.com/tfmr-therapist-directory%20): a directory of pro-choice therapists that have been vetted for TFMR-support  
    4. Warmline: a peer-run listening line alternative to crisis lines for those who are NOT in acute mental health distress but still need someone to talk to; these lines do not contact law enforcement. You can use their [national line](https://warmline.org/%20) or use the [state-specific directory](https://warmline.org/warmdir.html#directory%20)
