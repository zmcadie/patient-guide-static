Resources page

  1. Clinic Locators

    1. ineedana: A directory of vetted abortion clinics
    2. Abortion Finder: A directory of vetted abortion clinics

  2. Abortion funds and Practical Support Organizations

    1. National Network of Abortion Funds (NNAF): a network of nearly 100 abortion funds. You can search for abortion funds that cover your area or the area of your appointment.
    2. Chicago Abortion Fund: an abortion fund that provides financial and logistical support for people from the midwest or receiving their care in the midwest including residents of Illinois, Wisconsin, Nebraska, Arkansas and Iowa or anyone traveling to Illinois, Wisconsin, Iowa & Nebraska for care.
      1. The Chicago Abortion Fund also has a program, CARLA, specifically to support people seeking care in hospitals. CARLA is NOT restricted to the Midwest.
    3. National Abortion Federation: The National Abortion Hotline is the largest national, toll-free, multi-lingual Hotline for abortion provider information and financial assistance in the U.S. and Canada.

  3. Practical Support Organizations

    1. BRIDGID Alliance: a practical support organization that provides support for later abortion care
    2. Apiary: Apiary is a repository of practical support organizations. You can find one that covers the area of your appointment, or a national organization

  4. Abortion Story Telling

    1. Who Not When: an organization that provides information and advocacy for later abortion
    2. r/abortion : a reddit page run by volunteers who provide compassionate responses to stories and questions about abortion care.
    3. Shout your abortion : a repository of abortion stories
    4. We Testify : a repository of abortion stories

  5. Group Therapy

    1. Postpartum International After-Abortion support group: group therapy for people who have experienced abortion
    2. Post Partum International TMFR support group: group therapy for people who have experienced termination for medical reasons.

  6. Mental Health Supports

    1. Exhale pro-voice: a non-judgemental after-abortion support text line
    2. Pro-Choice Therapists : a directory of pro-choice therapists
    3. Termination For Medical Reasons Therapy: a directory of pro-choice therapists that have been vetted for TFMR-support
    4. Warmline: a peer-run listening line alternative to crisis lines for those who are NOT in acute mental health distress but still need someone to talk to; these lines do not contact law enforcement. You can use their national line or use the state-specific directory
